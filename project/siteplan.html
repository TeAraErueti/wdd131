Te Whare Tapa Whā Site Plan

Site Name

Te Whare Tapa Whā Aotearoa
The domain name will be TWTW-Aotearoa
This name represents a wellbeing site that focuses on the four walls of a house or in this situation, four domains of health and wellbeing, including one's connection to the land.

www.twtw-Aotearoa.co.nz

Site Purpose

The site provides information on a wellbeing model we use in New Zealand called Te Whare Tapa Whā.
The site will provide information on the foundations of this wellbeing model in hopes it may help others to not only be culturally aware but also help them with their own health and wellbeing.

Navigation

Te Whare Tapa Whā Taha Wairua Taha Hinengaro Taha Tinana Taha Whānau Whenua

Scenarios

What is an alternative approach to holistic wellbeing from a Māori perspective?
How does this approach to health and wellbeing apply to my life?
These scenarios can be tailored to many from various backgrounds. The approach to this wellbeing model applies to many of different ages and backgrounds, specifically those looking for a more holistic approach to overall health and wellbeing.

Color Schema

The colors I will be using in my web project are culture based.
I will opt for black, green, red, blue, brown and neutral colors.
The black will mainly be used for font color, red possibly for logos/ images, green for background and images, blue for header and footer, brown for borders and neutral colors throughout the project.
As I progress in my project I have considered tools such as coolors.co to explore variations of color to use.

Typography

The fonts I will be using are open sans or sans serif and inter.
I have gone for simple styled fonts as I want my target audience to be able to read my website project content clearly.
I will use my fonts for heading, body and footer.
I have contemplated fancier styled fonts for headings but do not want my target audience to get confused with different styles of fonts.

Headings Font - font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;

Normal Text/Paragraph Font - font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;
